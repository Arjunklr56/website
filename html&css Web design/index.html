FAMILY-ORIENTED CARE

Welcome to

Texas Sage Dentistry

Welcome to Texas Sage Dentistry where you'll find comprehensive dental treatments for the whole family in the heart of Historic Downtown McKinney. Our skilled and friendly dental team is all about providing high-quality, convenient dental . We offer general dentistry services in addition to cosmetic and restorative procedures like whitening, veneers, dental implants, and more. Our boutique dental office in McKinney, TX is a relaxed, welcoming environment where patients become like family. We look forward to meeting you soon!

Meet Our Team

Dr. Barret Davidson is thrilled to provide family and cosmetic dental services to patients in the communities located in and around McKinney, TX. As the owner of Texas Sage Dentistry, Dr. Davidson is equipped with advanced skills and expertise to perform general, cosmetic, and restorative dental procedures and treatments that meet your oral care needs. Dr. Davidson works alongside board-certified prosthodontist Dr. Eugene Dahl, a highly experienced family dentist who has been practicing in McKinney for many years. Together, they share a commitment to maintaining the highest standards of excellence. Our team takes a compassionate, personalized approach to care. We work hard to ensure that all of our patients have a positive, relaxed dental experience.
